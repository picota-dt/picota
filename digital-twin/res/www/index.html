How Picota Works

1. Define your model

Start by describing the structure of your system: what inputs it observes and what outputs you want it to estimate or predict. You’ll do this in Quassar.io using Picota’s own modeling language. This model becomes the blueprint for your Digital Twin.

2. Train from your data

Upload a dataset that matches your model. Picota automatically builds and trains a neural network based on your data. Once training is complete, the dataset is deleted. Your model stays, your data doesn’t.

3. Use your digital twin

Your trained model is now ready to make inferences. You can test it or integrate it via API. Every Digital Twin is versioned, traceable, and fully based on your custom structure.
